# seo

13 sub-skills and 8 subagents for complete SEO coverage.

*   `/seo audit` -- full site technical audit
*   `/seo page` -- single page deep analysis
*   `/seo content` -- E-E-A-T quality scoring
*   `/seo schema` -- JSON-LD structured data
*   `/seo geo` -- AI Overviews / GEO optimization
*   `/seo plan` -- strategic SEO planning
*   `/seo competitor` -- gap analysis
*   SEO Health Score (0-100) with 7 weighted categories
*   Industry detection: SaaS, e-commerce, local, publishers
